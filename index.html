Our Story

Why Handmade Matters

Crafted from the heart

Unlike mass-produced trinkets, our keepsakes are artfully handcrafted by a single artisan — me. Every detail, from hand-selecting your dried flowers to pouring crystal-clear resin, is done with intention and care. This artisanal process ensures your gift is as unique as your story.

It all began with my own wedding flowers. I couldn't bear to let them wilt away, so I taught myself to preserve them in resin. When friends saw the result, they wanted their own. That personal passion became The Craft Next Door — a studio where every creation begins with someone's cherished memory.
